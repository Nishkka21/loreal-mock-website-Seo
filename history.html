Brand History

L’Oréal Paris was founded in 1909 by Eugène Schueller. What started with a revolutionary hair dye formula has turned into the world’s largest cosmetics company.

  • 1909: Founded by Eugène Schueller.
  • 1928: First acquisition (Monsavon soap company).
  • 1973: Introduction of the slogan "Because I'm Worth It".
  • 1988: Became the world’s largest cosmetics company.
