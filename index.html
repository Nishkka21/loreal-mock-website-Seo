About L'Oréal Paris

Founded in 1909 by a young chemist, Eugène Schueller, L’Oréal Paris has been a trailblazer in the beauty industry. It began with a revolutionary hair dye formula and has evolved into a global beauty brand that empowers women and promotes innovation.

Our product range includes skincare, makeup, hair care, and well-being products. L’Oréal Paris is committed to sustainability, diversity, and scientific excellence.
