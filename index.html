About L'Oréal Paris

Founded in 1909 by a young chemist, Eugène Schueller, L’Oréal Paris has been a trailblazer in the beauty industry. It began with a revolutionary hair dye formula and has evolved into a global beauty brand that empowers women and promotes innovation.

Our product range includes skincare, makeup, hair care, and well-being products. L’Oréal Paris is committed to sustainability, diversity, and scientific excellence.

Our Journey

  • 1909: Founded by Eugène Schueller with a revolutionary hair dye formula.
  • 1928: L’Oréal makes its first acquisition by purchasing the soap company Monsavon.
  • 1935: Launched Ambre Solaire, the first filtered tanning oil.
  • 1973: The iconic slogan "Because I'm Worth It" was introduced.
  • 1988: Became the world’s largest cosmetics company.
  • 2006: Jean-Paul Agon became CEO and focused on global expansion.
  • Today: L’Oréal continues to lead with innovations in beauty products and sustainability.
